# Hook cheat sheets — the whole course in one pass

Every page in this course compresses its core insight into hooks — one-phrase handles built to survive interview pressure. These sheets collect all of them, day by day, in page order: roughly 370 hooks standing in for 87 pages of material.

## How to use these sheets

They are **not for learning** — a hook you never studied is trivia, not a handle. They're for the two moments when re-reading full pages is the wrong tool:

1. **End of each study day**: skim that day's sheet; any hook that doesn't instantly unfold into its full answer marks the page to revisit in your hour-10 buffer.
2. **The day before the interview**: one pass through all seven sheets, out loud, is a review of the entire course in about ninety minutes. Say the hook, then reconstruct the answer it compresses — the reconstruction is the drill, the hook is just the trigger (see [How to answer](../day0/01-how-to-answer.md) for the delivery technique).
