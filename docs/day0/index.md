# Day 0 — Interview craft

## Why this section exists

Seven days of this course build *what* to say. This short section is about *how* to say it — and about the half of an Amazon interview that isn't technical at all. It's called Day 0 because it's meant to be read **before Day 1** (about an hour), and then **re-read the day before the interview**, when its two pages matter most.

Unlike the technical days, nothing here needs 10 hours. What it does need — specifically the Leadership Principles page — is **personalization work only you can do**: the story scaffolds there are anchored to your real projects, but the numbers, names, and outcomes have to be filled in from your own memory, and that's worth doing days before the interview, not the night before.
